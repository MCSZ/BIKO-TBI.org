About the Brain Injury Knowledge Ontology KnowledgeBase (BIKObase)

BIKObase

Welcome to the page for BIKObase, a multi-species translational traumatic brain injury knowledgebase that assists the field in identifying the metadata in TBI research papers. All papers in BIKObase have been retrieved from PubMed, so they all have an associated PubMed ID. .
